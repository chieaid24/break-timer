# Productivity Timer 1.1.0

## New

- Reminders are now configurable. Right-click the tray icon and choose **Settings...** to change the break interval, the notification message, and the sound. Earlier builds fired a fixed 20-minute reminder with no settings.
- The interval accepts 1 to 1440 minutes. Saving a changed interval restarts the current countdown.
- The sound can be a built-in Windows sound, silent, or a custom WAV file.
- Settings persist in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\ProductivityTimer` alongside the logs and the last successful trigger.

## Changed

- The tray cigarette icon is larger and easier to read at small sizes.

## Notes

This release is unsigned, so Windows SmartScreen may show a warning. Click **More info**, confirm the filename is `ProductivityTimer-Setup.exe`, then click **Run anyway**. Do not disable SmartScreen. Managed computers may require administrator approval.
